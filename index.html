OVERVIEW

Embark on a captivating journey through the rich history and breathtaking landscapes of the Athens Riviera. Our road trip adventure promises an unforgettable exploration of iconic sites, including the majestic Temple of Poseidon in Sounio and the historic wonders of Athens itself. Get ready to immerse yourself in the timeless allure of ancient Greek culture and the stunning beauty of the Aegean Sea.

As we traverse the scenic coastal route along the Athens Riviera, you'll witness the fusion of modern luxury and ancient charm that defines this enchanting region. Our ultimate destination is the Temple of Poseidon at Cape Sounio, a masterpiece of classical architecture perched on the cliffs overlooking the azure waters of the Aegean. Feel the magic of standing where ancient Greeks once worshiped, while enjoying panoramic views that extend to the distant horizon.

After basking in the aura of Poseidon's temple, we'll return to Athens for a comprehensive city tour. Uncover the layers of history at the Acropolis, where the Parthenon stands as a testament to the glory of ancient Athens. Stroll through the charming Plaka district, with its labyrinthine streets and vibrant atmosphere, offering a taste of both tradition and modernity.

Join us on this road trip to Athens Riviera, where every twist and turn reveals a new chapter in the fascinating story of Greece. Immerse yourself in the wonders of the past, savor the present, and create memories that will linger long after the journey ends.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with a seamless full-day private tour, exploring the Athenian Riviera and Cape Sounio. Experience the invigorating breeze in your hair as you journey from your Athens hotel to the picturesque coast in a convertible car. Enjoy leisurely stops for swimming and capturing stunning moments. As the day unfolds, witness the breathtaking sunset at the iconic Temple of Poseidon in Cape Sounio. Benefit from hassle-free hotel pickup, travel in comfort in a convertible, and savor the freedom to explore at your own pace. Marvel at the enchanting views of the Temple of Poseidon as the sun sets, making this a truly unforgettable adventure.

THE TRIP

Embark on a scenic journey along the renowned "Athens Riviera," starting with pickups from our guests' locations. We'll traverse captivating spots such as Vouliagmeni Lake, Limanakia, Mavro Lithari, and Saronida before reaching the majestic Temple of Poseidon at Sounio. Along the way, indulge in opportunities for swimming, photography, and soaking in the breathtaking views. Ample time awaits to explore the Temple and relish the mesmerizing sunset. Our return follows the same picturesque route, ensuring a convenient drop-off at our guests' chosen locations.
